Mushroom Rice

--> cook rice
--> wash mushrooms by taking only head and cut vertically
--> Fry mushrooms(with no oil) --> water will come out --> Add some salt and pepper --> again fry for 2-3 mins --> put this aside
--> put pan --> add biryani related --> add onions --> fry them --> add tomatoes --> fry them --> add masalas i.e., coriander, cumin, biryani --> fry them --> add turmeric, salt, chilli powder --> fry 
--> add rice --> wait for 10 mins
